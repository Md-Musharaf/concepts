# Listening and Active Communication

## Question 1: Active Listening

1. Give full attention to the speaker.
2. Avoid interrupting.
3. Maintain eye contact.
4. Ask questions for clarity.
5. Listen to understand, not reply.
6. Summarize important points.

---

## Question 2: Reflective Listening

1. Listen carefully to words and emotions.
2. Repeat the message in your own words.
3. Confirm understanding with the speaker.
4. Observe body language and tone.
5. Avoid judging or arguing.

---

## Question 3: Obstacles in Listening

1. Distractions like mobile phones.
2. Losing focus during long talks.
3. Thinking about replies too early.
4. Stress or tiredness.

---

## Question 4: Improving Listening

1. Focus fully on the speaker.
2. Take notes in important discussions.
3. Ask questions when confused.
4. Avoid interruptions.
5. Practice patience.

---

## Question 5: Passive Communication

I become passive when I feel nervous or want to avoid conflict.

---

## Question 6: Aggressive Communication

I become aggressive when I am frustrated or stressed.

---

## Question 7: Passive Aggressive Communication

I may use sarcasm or stay silent when upset instead of speaking directly.

---

## Question 8: Assertive Communication

1. Speak clearly and respectfully.
2. Listen carefully before responding.
3. Stay calm during disagreements.
4. Express feelings honestly.
5. Respect others’ opinions.
