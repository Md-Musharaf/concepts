# Learning Process

## Question 1
### What is the Feynman Technique?

The Feynman Technique is a method of learning by explaining concepts in simple words.

---

## Question 2
### Most interesting idea from Barbara Oakley’s TED Talk

The most interesting idea was that the brain learns better when we take breaks and switch between focused and relaxed thinking.

---

## Question 3
### What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

1. Active mode is focused thinking used while solving problems.
2. Diffused mode is relaxed thinking where the brain connects ideas in the background.

---

## Question 4
### Steps to approach a new topic

1. Break the skill into smaller parts.
2. Learn enough to self-correct mistakes.
3. Remove distractions.
4. Practice regularly for at least 20 hours.

---

## Question 5
### Actions to improve my learning process

1. Practice daily with full concentration.
2. Reduce phone and social media distractions.
3. Take notes and revise concepts regularly.
4. Focus on understanding concepts deeply.
5. Try to explain concepts in my own words.
6. Complete assignments on time.
7. Practice coding consistently.
